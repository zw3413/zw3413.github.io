Attention Deficit Hyperactivity Disorder Diagnosis using non-linear univariate and multivariate EEG measurements: a preliminary study

  1. Most of these studies conducted on children with ADHD have reported that they have a
    • reduced power in fast frequency bands, such as alpha and beta,
    • an increased power in slow frequency bands, such as delta and theta

qEEG measurement of Theta/Beta Ratio(TBR)
- TBR is a utilization approved by U.S. Food and Drug Administration(FDA) as a robust diagnostic tool for patients who may have ADHD.
- TBR is the ratio of the amount of theta activity divided by the amount of beta activity.
- both theta and beta are measured at the scalp vertext site Cz.
- recent studies did not find a significant difference in TBR between two groups and they proposed that TBR may not be a dependable maker of ADHD.

  1. Non-linear analysis to EEG signals provides information about the complex dynamics of underlying neuronal networks.
    • Approximate Entropy (AppEn)
    • Fractal Demension (FD)
    • Lyapunov exponent and wavelet entropy

Entropy is a prevailing way to quantify the regularity of systems.

  • Shannon Entropy(ShanEn) was used in epilepsy diagnosis, sleep stages, Alzheimer, and brain injury after cardiac arrest.
  • Sample Entropy (SampEn), was used fewly in biomedical studies, but for analyzing epileptic seizures EEG background activity of Alzheimer’s disease and Magnetoencephalography (MEG) activity of ADHD patients.
  • Dispersion entropy (DispEn),
  1. Traditional approaches for estimating complexity of biological signals
  • Lampel-Ziv complexity (LZC), fail to explain multiple time-scales inherent in such time series.
    LZC是一种基于压缩算法的非线性度量，衡量一个时间序列中新模式出现的频率。它反应的是序列的复杂程度或信息熵的近似。
    比如：周期性序列010101 LZC低， 随机序列010110100101 LZC高

多时间尺度：EEG往往包含多个频率成分或不同时长的动态过程，比如：
- 快速变化(如 α\alphaα 波)
- 缓慢波动(如 δ\deltaδ 波)
- 长期趋势(比如睡眠周期)

为什么LZC午发很好的解释时间序列中固有的多时间尺度结构
- 不考虑局部时间结构
- 也不区分不同时间尺度上的规律性或随机性
- 本质上是全局的复杂度度量
即 LZC无法识别一个信号中同时存在的快慢变化模式，不能区分在短期内简单、长期内复杂这样的结构。

  • **Multiscale Sample Entropy (MSE)**多尺度样本熵 is a method overcome above limitation

    • potent, prevalent
    • but not able to manifest the dynamics across the channels. 不能发现通道间的动力学

  • **Multivariate Sample Entropy (mvSE)**多元样本熵 leading to an extension of MSE to multivariate signals, that is mvMSE.

    • a tool for complexity analysis of mutichannel data and has obtained much attention in biomedical fields.

Region-Wise Brain Response Classification of ASD Children Using EEG and BiLSTM RNN

  1. Autism spectrum disorder (ASD 自闭症谱系障碍)
    ASD is a neurodevelopmental disorder (神经发育障碍) characterized by impairment in sensory modulation. (特征是感觉调节能力受损) These sensory modulation deficits would ultimately lead them to difficulties in adaptive behavior(适应性行为) and intellectual functioning(智力功能). The purpose of this study was to observe changes in the nervous system with response to auditory/visual and only audio stimuli in children with autism and typically developing (TD) through electroencephalography(EEG).
    The neural dynamics could be understood by non-linear analysis of the EEG signal.
    this research reveal the underlying nonlinear EEG dynamics, recurrence quantification analysis(RQA) is applied

  2. non-linear feature and RQA
    The basic criterion for nonlinear analysis is to represent trajectories in phase space. Recurrences of system trajectories in the phase space are the best method for systems with nonlinear and nonstationary behavior.
    非线性分析最基础的准则是表示在相空间的轨迹。 系统轨迹在相空间的重复出现是分析带有非线性非稳定系统的方法。
    This representation reveals the underlying information of the complex systems. 这种表示揭示了复杂系统的底层信息。
    The recurrence plot(RP) provides useful perceptions of the dynamic systems. 复现图提供了对于动态系统的有用的认知。
    These RPs have to be visually interpreted with the patterns based on the observer’s views, hence, interpretation differs from one person to another. To overcome such disadvantages of RP, complex measures are derived from RP which are denoted as recurrence quantification annlysis (RQA)

RQA features were extracted in various researches such as classification of different sleep stages, epilepsy identification, differentiating ASD and epileptic signals, and drowsiness detection.

Some studies have used RQA in detecting cardiovascular problems and motor developmental problems. RQA is concluded to be a suitable tool for chaotic nonlinear signals like EEG.

The parameters which play a crucial role in RP are embedding dimension distance metric, and neighborhood selection.

  1. Classifier
    A classifier must be selected to consider both past and present sequences for classification.
    RNN is considered for classification.
    RNN is not limited to the fixed-length input.
    problems in RNN
    • short-term memory
    • vanishing gradient problem
    • it does not perform well with long sequences.
      To overcome such limitations, LSTM is used with RNN. Interocular-modulated attentional LSTM network distinguished the subgroups of ASD at an accuracy of 66.5%
      Identification of ASD using an eye-tracking technique with specific LSTM network at an accuracy of 83%.

3 main modules in this study:
- Preprocessing of EEG signal
- Features extracted using various parameter combinations (cosine distance metric) in RQA and RNN with BiLSTM for classification.
- Brain region with a higher response fo the presented stimuli is identified.(对所呈现刺激反应更强烈的大脑区域被识别出来)

  2. Feature Extraction

    1. RQA (Recurrent Quantification Analysis)
      • Dynamic characteristics of any time series can be computed by a graphical tool representation known as recurrence plots. (任意时序动态特征可以使用一个叫做递归图的图形工具表示计算)
      • RP represents the number of times a particular state recurs in phase space. RP表示一个特定状态在相空间重复出现的次数
      • This graphical tool initially was developed to provide a better understanding of the high-dimensional dynamic system as it was challenging to visualize and analyze in those phase space trajectories. 这个图形化工具开始是被开发用来理解高维度动态系统，因为它们很难在这些相空间轨迹中被分析和可视化。
      • Advantages of RP
        • function well on noisy data, nonlinear data, and also short datasets.
        • The preservation of the fundamental structures of the initial trajectory is certain, only if it satisfies the condition m > 2d +1 in which d is the dimension of the attractor and m is the embedding dimension. 若要保留原始动力系统轨迹的基本结构(拓扑特征)，就必须满足这个条件 m>2d+1, d 是吸引子位数(系统真实的自由度或复杂度)， m 是嵌入维数。
          • Taken 定理： 一个未知动力系统（如EEG）的相空间轨迹，可以通过延迟嵌入从单变量信号中重构出来，只要选择的嵌入维度m>2d或更严格 m>2d+1
          • 在实际使用RQA（基于Recurrence Plot）时，我们通常只有EEG的单通道时间序列，但真实的大脑系统时高维的非线性系统。
          • 为了用RQA捕捉系统的轨迹结构，我们先对信号做延迟嵌入： X(t)=[x(t),x(t+τ),x(t+2τ),⋅,x(t+(m−1)τ)]X(t) = [x(t), x(t+\tau), x(t+2\tau), \cdot, x(t+(m-1)\tau)] X(t)=[x(t),x(t+τ),x(t+2τ),⋅,x(t+(m−1)τ)]
          • 只有当嵌入维数m足够大(>2d+1)时，这个嵌入空间中的轨迹才能反应真实系统的吸引子结构。
          • 否则，RP图中就可能出现虚假的轨迹相交、结构破坏、误判复现行为。
          • 总结： 使用RQA的前提时通过时间延迟嵌入还原系统轨迹， 而这种轨迹结构(如吸引子)只有在嵌入维度m>2d+1时才可保留，这是RQA有效捕捉非线性动力学行为的理论基础之一。
          • 在实际应用中，由于d(吸引子维数)通常未知，我们会通过：
            • False Nearest Neighbors(FNN) 方法估计合适的m
            • 或经验设置m = 3~10来平衡计算与保真度
      • 四个核心RQA指标 RR,DET,L,ENTR，分别刻画了系统的稳定性、可预测性、动态持续性与行为复杂度，是非线性时间序列分析的重要工具
        • RR （Recurrence Rate 复现率），所有复现点占总点数的比例，动力学解释：系统状态回到以前状态的频率，反映稳定性/周期性强度
        • DET （Determinism 决定性） 所有对角线复现点占总复现点的比例，动力学解释：轨迹是否按规律重复演化，反应系统的可预测性
        • L （Average Diagnoal Line Length 平均对角线长度）, 所有对角线长度的平均值， 动力学解释：可预测行为的持续时间，越长表示动态过程越持久一致。
        • ENTR （Entropy 对角线熵） 所有对角线长度的分布熵，动力学解释：行为模式的多样性与复杂读，高熵说明轨迹模式多、系统复杂或混沌。
      • 在RP中
        • 点状分布 –> 随机系统 –> 低DET,低L, 低ENTR
        • 写现状 –> 可预测系统(如周期、准周期) –> 高DET， 高L
        • 斑块状或网格 –> 稳定吸引子结构 –> 高RR
        • 碎片化斜线 –> 混沌 –> 中等DET, 高ENTR
      • EEG例子
        • RR高： 表示脑电信号状态经常复现，可能处于某种稳定节律状态(如 α\alphaα波)
        • DET高： 表示信号烟花遵循某种可预测轨迹，可能是清醒、任务状态
        • L高： 表示脑电状态连续性强，波形持久
        • ENTR高： 表示信号复杂多变，可能反映混沌、意识变化或认知负荷高
      • 应用
        • setting up the embedding dimension and time delay,
        • a suitable neighborhood method, 2 neighborhood selections were tested such as FAN and fixed radius method.
          • FAN(Fixed Amount of Nearest neighbors)邻域选择法，它适用于构建RP时的一种”距离阈值选择策略”，特别适用于非线性数据 FAN neighborhood selection chooses a radius hence it contains a fixed number of closed states, FAN 邻域选择法会为每个状态点选择一个半径 RiR_iRi​, 这个半径是这样的:它恰好包含固定数量的“近邻状态”。 The radius changes for each closed state (I = 1, …., N), where N is the number of closed states and the neighborhood of xi and xj does not have to be the same (Ri ≠ Rji). FAN is significantly used in nonlinear data and for the comparison of 2 different systems.
          • Fixed radius method chooses threshold ensuring Rij = Rji. Selection of threshold depends on 2 conditions such as < 10% of the maximum diameter of phase space and for classification/signal detection purpose, the threshold is set at a range between 20% and 40% of standard deviation (σ) of the signal
    • Electrodes
      • Electrodes selected for research were Fp1 , T3, T5, Q1, Fp2, F8, F4, Fz and O2
      • From each electrode, 10 RQA features were extracted.
      • 20 children in each group, with 5 different stimuli, 2-min for each duration

    • sample rebalance with weight
    • SVM with RBF kernel, using bayes optimizer get the parameter of BoxConstraint and KernelScale, with 5-fold
    • metrics: accuracy, sensitivity, specificity, F1-Score ,AUC
