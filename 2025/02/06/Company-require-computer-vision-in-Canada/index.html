Computer Vision

Skill Development

Deep Learning & Vision Models:

Master CNNs, Transformers (ViTs), GANs, and self-supervised learning.

Key Libraries & Tools:

OpenCV, PyTorch, TensorFlow, ONNX, NVIDIA TensorRT.

3D Vision & SLAM:

If interested in robotics/AR/VR, study 3D reconstruction & LiDAR processing.

Projects & Portfolio

Build Real-World Projects:

Object detection, face recognition, medical image analysis.

Kaggle Competitions:

Participate in computer vision challenges.

GitHub & Open-Source:

Contribute to frameworks like OpenMMLab, Detectron2.

Companies in 3D Scanning & Machine Vision Operating in Canada

1. LMI Technologies

  • Overview: A global leader in 3D scanning, machine vision, and smart sensor technology for industrial automation and quality inspection.
  • Headquarters: Burnaby, British Columbia, Canada
  • Notable Products: Gocator 3D smart sensors, FocalSpec 3D line confocal sensors
  • Industries: Manufacturing, robotics, automotive, electronics, logistics
  • Website: LMI Technologies

2. Teledyne DALSA

  • Overview: Designs and manufactures digital imaging products for industrial, scientific, and medical applications.
  • Headquarters: Waterloo, Ontario, Canada
  • Industries: Semiconductor, electronics, medical imaging, automation
  • Website: Teledyne DALSA

3. Cognex Corporation (Canada)

  • Overview: Provides a wide range of machine vision products, including image sensors, 2D and 3D vision systems, and software solutions.
  • Canadian Presence: Offices in Canada, serving various industrial sectors.
  • Industries: Automotive, logistics, electronics, pharmaceuticals
  • Website: Cognex Canada

4. Jesse Garant Metrology Center

  • Overview: A part inspection company providing non-destructive testing (NDT) and metrology services using advanced imaging equipment.
  • Headquarters: Windsor, Ontario, Canada
  • Industries: Aerospace, automotive, manufacturing
  • Website: Jesse Garant Metrology

5. Vista Solutions

  • Overview: Specializes in providing turnkey machine vision solutions, services, and integration across various industries.
  • Canadian Presence: Based in Canada, serving clients across North America.
  • Industries: Industrial automation, quality control, robotics
  • Website: Vista Solutions

6. ControlSoft Canada

  • Overview: Offers comprehensive machine vision engineering solutions for industrial applications, focusing on quality control, inspection, and automation processes.
  • Canadian Presence: Operates within Canada.
  • Industries: Manufacturing, food processing, pharmaceuticals
  • Website: ControlSoft Canada
